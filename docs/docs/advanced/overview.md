# Advanced Topics

Ready to look under the hood? Advanced does not mean off-limits. These recipes
help you investigate bugs, tune builds, spot memory leaks, tackle numerical
work, and let programs handle more than one task at a time.

## Choose Your Next Challenge

- **Make the compiler work for you:** Explore [conditional compilation](conditional-compilation.md)
  and useful [FPC switches](fpc-switches.md).
- **Track down troublesome bugs:** [Debug with Lazarus](debugging.md) and
  [find heap memory leaks](find-heap-memory-leaks.md).
- **Crunch some numbers:** Meet [LMath](lmath.md) or explore the
  [NumLib examples](numlib.md).
- **Do more at once:** Decide whether you [need multithreading](multi-threading-intro.md),
  then meet [`TThread`](tthread-i-basic.md) or try [ezthreads](ezthreads.md).

Pick the challenge that sounds useful today; you do not need to master the
whole section before trying one recipe.

!!! tip "Need the tools first?"

    [Set up the Free Pascal Compiler and Lazarus IDE](../basics/install.md), then
    return when you are ready to go deeper.
